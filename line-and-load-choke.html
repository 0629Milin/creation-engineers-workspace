Input and Output Choke

CREATION ENGINEERS is one of the most renowned manufacturers of Input and Output Choke, also known as Input and Output Choke, used before and after Variable Frequency Drive (VFD) respectively. Our RL Line/Load Reactors are best-in-class power quality units with a long history of proven performance. Rugged and robust, they absorb power line disturbances that can damage or shut down VFDs and other sensitive equipment.

They work on both the line side and load side, providing an easy solution to reduce nuisance tripping, harmonic distortion, and long lead effects. Improved power quality, enhanced productivity, and complete peace of mind are achieved with RL Line/Load Reactors.
